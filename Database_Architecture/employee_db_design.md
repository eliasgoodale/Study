# Practice Employee Database

    We have 6 tables to represent company data:

*   Employees
*   Locations
*   Salaries
*   Postal Codes
*   Locations
*   Departments

> We will assume that every employee only works at one location and in one department for simplicity

Every Employee has: 
 *  1 Department (FK)
 *  1 Location (FK)
 *  1 salary (FK)
 *  Many customers (FK)
 *  first name
 *  last name
 *  gender 
 *  hire date
 *  photo
 *  Address
 *  Birthday
